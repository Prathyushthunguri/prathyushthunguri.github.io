Fluid Reference Guide (D471)
IRM 902 Replaces ASTM Oil No.2. Light petroleum oil. Low swell test fluid. Used for EO suffix (mild oil exposure).
IRM 903 Replaces ASTM Oil No.3. Severe petroleum oil. High aniline point. Primary class-rating oil. Used for EO suffix (harsh oil exposure).
Fuel A 100% isooctane. Represents low-aromatic gasoline. Mild swelling agent. EF suffix test fluid.
Fuel B 70% isooctane + 30% toluene. Simulates regular unleaded gasoline. Moderate aromatic content. EF suffix.
Fuel C 50% isooctane + 50% toluene. Severe aromatic fuel blend. Simulates high-aromatic/premium fuels. EF suffix.
Fuel D 100% toluene. Extreme aromatic solvent. Most aggressive fuel test fluid. EF suffix — special cases.
Water Distilled water immersion, 70h @ 100°C. EA suffix. Checks hydrolytic stability.
Water/Glycol 50/50 vol% distilled water + ethylene glycol. Simulates coolant/antifreeze. EA suffix.
