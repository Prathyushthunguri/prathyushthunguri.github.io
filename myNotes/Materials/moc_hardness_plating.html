MOC-Specific Coating Rules


Carbon Steel / WCB

Most versatile base — accepts hard chrome, HVOF, EN, flame spray and hard-facing overlay. Pre-surface treatment (blast to Sa 2.5) mandatory before thermal spray.

Stainless Steel (304 / 316)

Avoid hex-chrome plating. Use HVOF WC-CoCr or EN+P. TiN PVD is ideal for precision components. Check galvanic compatibility of coating in service environment.

Duplex / Super Duplex

High base hardness reduces need for coating. When required, HVOF is the preferred method. Avoid processes requiring temperatures above 280°C to prevent phase transformation.

Nickel Alloys (Hastelloy / Inconel)

HVOF WC-CoCr is the preferred system. EN+P acceptable for mild service. Avoid hard chrome — galvanic and adhesion issues reported.

Hardox Sleeve / WCB Casting

Always use LH 108 or equivalent hard-facing electrodes at welded interfaces. HVOF or ceramic spray on bore surface gives outstanding slurry resistance. Rubber lining suitable only for fine particle (<3 mm) abrasive slurry.

High Chrome Iron / Ni-Hard

Self-sufficient for abrasion. Coatings generally not applied. Localised repairs using Stoody 102 or EFe-Cr4 overlay electrodes. Inspect for sub-surface cracking before overlay.

Hardness vs. Abrasive Particle — Selection Principle

  • A material surface must be harder than the abrasive particle to resist wear
  • The table below shows minimum coating requirements based on common abrasive hardness values

Abrasive Media Approx. Hardness Assessment Recommended Coating
Coal / Soft Minerals<100 HVStandard CS / SS surface adequateNo coating required (optional)
Limestone / Calcite~160 HVMild protection neededHard Chrome or EN+P
Iron Ore / Magnetite~600 HVHard-facing overlay neededLH 108, Stoody 102, Hard Chrome
Sand / Silica (SiO2)~1100 HVHigh-hardness coating essentialHVOF WC-Co, TiN, Ceramic
Alumina (Al2O3)~2000 HVOnly ceramic coatings exceed thisPlasma Spray Al2O3, Cr2O3
Silicon Carbide (SiC)~2500 HVExtreme — no coating fully resistsCr2O3 plasma + rubber lining (velocity isolation)
For PN 350 WCB + Hardox application: Silica-based abrasives (~1100 HV) will rapidly erode any uncoated CS or SS surface. Recommended solution — HVOF WC-Co on bore face + LH 108 hard-facing at weld joint = complete protection.
