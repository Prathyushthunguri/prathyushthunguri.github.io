Stair Climbing Walker

Purpose for choosing project

  • Our project addresses the challenge of climbing stairs, particularly for individuals facing mobility issues, such as the elderly or disabled, who may struggle to lift a walker while ascending.
  • This is needed because not all places have lifts or escalators.

Summary

  • We introduced a motorized system with a triangular wheel configuration to enhance the existing solution.
  • This design ensures that at any given moment, one wheel is positioned on the current step, another is ascending or descending, and the third is in the air.
  • This enables a seamless stair-climbing experience without the need to lift the walker.

Procedure

  • The motor, connected to the wheel shaft through a gear mechanism, powers the triangular wheel setup located at the front of the stair climber.
  • We manufactured a robust and efficient triangular wheel system using laser-cut steel sheets.
  • For user control, we incorporated a button to toggle the motor on and off, allowing elderly or disabled individuals to navigate stairs effortlessly with their walkers.
  • We also implemented a low-speed setting to ensure stability and prevent balance issues during the ascent or descent.
