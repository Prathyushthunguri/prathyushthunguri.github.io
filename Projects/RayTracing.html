Ray Tracing Engine

Ray Tracing simulates the behavior of light rays as they travel through a scene, interacting with surfaces, materials, and light sources.

Project Description

  • First we divided the screen to capture image into pixels.
  • From each pixel a light ray starts parallel to normal of the plane of screen.
  • We have given limit on number of reflections a ray can make.
  • When ray intersects a object, the intensity of color of object it absorbs reduces as number of reflections increase.
  • When number of intersections reach the limit we add the intensities of the objects to form a final image for that pixel.
  • We then ran a loop for all the pixels doing the above thing.

Objects considered

  • Written algebraic equations for reflection of light rays for these particular objects and to calculate the intensity absorbed by the light ray.
  • Triange, Sphere, Plane
